My Favorite Scene

In all of Your Name, I think my favorite scene out of all is where they write on each other's hands. This scene is my favorite as it is cute and also sad at the same time. They were forgetting each other little by little and they said they would write each other's names on their hands. But Taki instead writes "I love you" on Mitsuha and eventually forget each other.

What is Your Name?

Your Name is a Japanese animated movie made by Makoto Shinkai. His other works include Weathering With You, The Garden of Words, and Suzume.

Why Watch Your Name?

Your Name is a Japanese animated romance movie about a boy from 2016 and a girl from 2013. The main plot is that they share a magical connection when they start swapping bodies; Taki and Mitsuha have to live each other's lives. But things get complicated once they try to meet each other in person.
